Simple Static Site — Contact

A single-file static website with an embedded contact form that posts to FormSubmit. Clean, accessible and responsive.

Single file: index.html
No external libs
Accessible form
How it works

When the form is submitted it will POST to FormSubmit and then show a client-side success message while the browser follows the action. You can add _next hidden input to redirect to a thank-you page if desired.

Made for testing & quick demos.

Fill the form and click Send. Required fields are marked.
